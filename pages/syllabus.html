Course Description

This course introduces students to statistical methods commonly used in agricultural and plant science research. Topics include descriptive statistics, probability distributions, hypothesis testing, analysis of variance, regression analysis, and experimental design. Students will learn to use statistical software (R/RStudio) for data analysis and interpretation.

Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of this course, students will be able to:

  • Apply appropriate statistical methods to analyze agricultural data
  • Design and conduct statistical analyses using R/RStudio
  • Interpret statistical results in the context of agricultural research
  • Communicate statistical findings effectively through written reports
  • Evaluate the validity and limitations of statistical analyses
